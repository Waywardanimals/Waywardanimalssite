Underdog Success Stories

From chaos to cuddle-bugs: Meet the legends of Mayhem’s Home.

Wiley -AKA- Land Whale

Featured in: the Mayhem Times

Wiley was a gift for an elderly woman before the passing of her husband. A precious last memory, she was permitted to do what every wild puppy would do when over loved and under disciplined. When her rambunctious ways caused her owner injury, she had to buckle down and enter sidekick training. Sadly, during her training, Wiley's owner passed away before she could take her rightful place. Her trainer, an autistic superhero in training, offered to take her in, train her up, and have a sidekick for life. So now Wiley spends her days with an Antihero called Anarchy... he's the brains behind the code!

Katie -AKA- Land Seal

Adopted! Congratulations to her new family

Katie was a villains dream. Her voice could level a nervous system with one seal-like wail from her oversized snout. Facing prison for her loud mouth ways, Katie came to us for rehab, where she met her hero, a survivor building a new life free from control. Now, she’s known for her gentle cuddles and has a home at last, but not before surprising everyone with a litter of 8 pups after swearing she was spayed for years. Katie is a liar, some habits are too hard to break! She's officially fixed now.

Alder & Douglas -AKA- The Spider Twins

Still looking for their superhero assignment

The wall-climbing,roof-dwellers who are always on the radar of some agency. Counter-surfing, hole digging, couch squatters... Almost straight 2 years of chaos, until they decided to mellow into good boys, almost. If you don't mind a little garbage theft, these boys could be the perfect sidekicks... Could your home be their next adventure?
