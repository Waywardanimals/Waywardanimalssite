Underdog Success Stories

From chaos to cuddle-bugs: Meet the legends of Mayhem’s Home.

Wiley -AKA- Land Whale

Wiley was a gift for an elderly woman before the passing of her husband. A precious last memory, she was permitted to do what every wild puppy would do when over loved and under diciplined, bounced around and cause chaos. When her handler was injured , her behavior had to go. Enter, Mayhem's Home pilot training program. We took in Wiley, worked with her and trained her to be a settled, focused, family member in belief that she's be going home in a few weeks. When her handler passed away unexpectedly, Wiley became an ESA for one of our volunteers. She grew from an unruly lab puppy into a calming sidekick.

Katie -AKA- Land Seal

Katie came from a family threatened with citations over her howl. Her voice was that of villains dreams, she could level a nervous system with one seal like wail from her oversized snout. While in training, she was connected to a new handler. Her handler, a survivor finding her way after trauma, took her in. However, in a surprise twist Katie gave birth to eight pups after swearing she was spayed years ago. Katie is a liar! Two of her pups —the Spider Twins: Alder and Douglas—stayed on here for training and assignments.

Alder & Douglas -AKA- The Spider Twins

The wall-climbing roof dwellers. Counter-surfing, hole digging, couch squatters... the both of 'em! Almost straight 2 years of chaos, until they decided to mellow into good boys, almost.... (garbage theft is the latest antics!). Late blooming nuisances reluctantly turned into sidekicks waiting for assignment.
